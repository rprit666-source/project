Every Stay Begins With a Journey

We travel the lengths of the country to find not just places, but stories waiting to be lived.

Our Quest for the Unique

Artisan Stays was born from a simple desire: to move beyond the generic and embrace the authentic. We believe the most memorable moments are found off the beaten path. Our quest is to uncover these hidden gems and share them with you.

The Path We Paved

The Spark (2022)

A frustrating search for a unique vacation home sparked an idea: What if there was a place for stays with soul?

First Discovery (2023)

We discovered our first property, a secluded farmhouse in Gujarat, setting the standard for all future stays.

The Artisan Standard (2024)

We defined our core principles: every property must excel in design, comfort, and authentic experience.

Our Promise (Today)

Our journey continues. We promise to keep exploring, curating, and bringing you the very best of India's hidden stays.
